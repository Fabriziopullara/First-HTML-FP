Pasta al forno with meat souce

How to make pasta al forno:

    Ingredients:

  1. Pasta
  2. Meat souce
  3. Cheese
  4. Salt
  5. Pepper
  6. Tomato

    Preparation:

  1. Boil water
  2. Add salt
  3. Boil until al dente
  4. Put pasta in a casserole
  5. Add souse
  6. Add cheese on top
  7. Bake it for 25 minutes, until cruchy on the top
